## Suggested ordering summary

`PR-1 → PR-2 → PR-3/4` (unbreak) → `PR-5…PR-10` (correctness, parallelizable) → `PR-11/12/13` (bump guardrails) → `PR-30a` (dangerous doc fixes) → `PR-15 → PR-16/17/18 → PR-19 → PR-20` (neutrality, strictly in that order) → `PR-14, PR-21…PR-29` (hardening/structure, opportunistic) → `PR-30b` (final doc pass).

The neutrality repackaging (PR-19/20) is deliberately sequenced **after** the path-resolution and policy work (PR-15/17) and before any third instrument is forked; the version-decoupling work (PR-8/11/12/14) is front-loaded because the next stack bump is a matter of time and the guardrails are cheap.
